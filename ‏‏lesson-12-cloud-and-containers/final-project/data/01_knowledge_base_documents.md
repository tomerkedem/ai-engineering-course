## Document 1: Return Policy

**Short description:**  
How and when customers can return items for a refund or exchange.

**Policy content:**

Our return policy allows customers to return items within **30 days of delivery** as long as the product is unused and in its original packaging. Items must be in resalable condition with all tags attached.

**Eligibility:**
- Return window: 30 days from the delivery date (as shown on your order confirmation or tracking).
- Product must be unused, unopened (where applicable), and in original packaging.
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.

**Exclusions:**
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.
- Digital products (e.g., e-books, software downloads) are non-returnable once accessed.
- Personalized or custom-made items are non-returnable unless defective.

**Process:** Initiate a return from your account under "Orders" or contact customer support. You will receive a return label. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the item.

## Document 2: Refund Policy

**Short description:**  
How refunds are processed and when customers can expect their money back.

**Policy content:**

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at purchase. Once we receive your returned item and confirm it meets our return policy, we process the refund within **5–7 business days**. It may take an additional 3–10 business days for your bank or card issuer to post the credit.

**Refund amount:**
- Full refund: original purchase price (excluding original shipping, unless the return is due to our error or a defective product).
- Partial refund: If an item is returned used or damaged, we may issue a partial refund or deny the refund.

**Exceptions:**
- Gift cards and store credit are non-refundable.
- If you paid with multiple methods, we refund in the reverse order of how they were charged.

**Chargebacks:** If you dispute the charge with your bank before contacting us, we may suspend your account until the dispute is resolved. We encourage you to contact us first so we can resolve the issue.

## Document 3: Exchange Policy

**Short description:**  
How to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product.

**Policy content:**

Exchanges are subject to the same eligibility rules as returns: within **30 days of delivery**, unused and in original packaging. You may exchange for a different size, color, or variant of the same product, or for another product of equal or lesser value.

**Process:**
1. Request an exchange through your account or customer support.
2. If the new item costs more, you pay the difference. If it costs less, we refund the difference to your original payment method.
3. We ship the replacement once we receive your returned item, or in some cases we can ship first (subject to account standing and product availability).

**Limitations:**
- Exchanges are not available for clearance or final sale items.
- Digital products cannot be exchanged.
- We do not guarantee availability of the requested size or color; if unavailable, we will offer a refund instead.

## Document 4: Order Cancellation

**Short description:**  
When and how orders can be cancelled.

**Policy content:**

Orders can only be cancelled if the order status is **Pending** (not yet shipped). Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled; you may return it after delivery according to our return policy.

**How to cancel:**
- Go to "My Orders" in your account, select the order, and click "Cancel order" if the option is available.
- Or contact customer support with your order number. We will cancel and refund the full amount if the order has not yet entered our fulfillment process.

**Timing:**
- Cancellation requests are processed during business hours. Orders that ship the same day may already be in progress; we will attempt to cancel but cannot guarantee it.
- Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3–5 business days.

**Pre-orders and back-ordered items:** You may cancel a pre-order or back-ordered item at any time before it ships. No restocking fee applies.

## Document 5: Shipping Policy

**Short description:**  
Domestic shipping options, timeframes, and costs.

**Policy content:**

We offer standard and express shipping within the continental United States. Orders placed before 2:00 PM (local warehouse time) on business days typically ship the same day; orders placed after that or on weekends/holidays ship the next business day.

**Shipping options (domestic):**
- **Standard:** 5–7 business days. Free on orders over $50; otherwise $5.99.
- **Express:** 2–3 business days. $12.99.
- **Overnight:** 1 business day. $24.99 (available for most items; some restrictions apply).

**Delivery:** We use major carriers (e.g., USPS, FedEx, UPS). You will receive a tracking number once the order ships. Delivery dates are estimates and not guaranteed; delays can occur due to weather or carrier issues.

**Multiple items:** If your order contains multiple items, they may ship in separate packages. You will see all tracking numbers in your order details.

## Document 6: International Shipping

**Short description:**  
Shipping to countries outside the United States.

**Policy content:**

We ship to **Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and most countries in the European Union**. We do not currently ship to all countries; at checkout, enter your address to see if delivery is available to your location.

**Costs and delivery times:**
- International shipping is calculated at checkout based on destination and weight.
- Delivery typically takes **10–21 business days** depending on the country and customs processing.
- Customers are responsible for any **customs duties, taxes, or import fees** charged by their country. We do not collect these at checkout unless required by law (e.g., certain EU countries).

**Restrictions:**
- Some products cannot be shipped internationally (e.g., certain electronics, items with batteries, or restricted categories). The product page will indicate if international shipping is not available.
- Returns from international addresses follow our standard return policy; the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

## Document 7: Damaged Products

**Short description:**  
What to do if your order arrives damaged.

**Policy content:**

If your order arrives damaged, we want to make it right. Please contact us within **7 days of delivery** with your order number and a description of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.

**Process:**
1. Do not discard the product or packaging until we have reviewed your case.
2. Contact customer support with: order number and description of damage.
3. We may ask you to return the damaged item using a prepaid label we provide, or we may approve a refund/replacement without return in clear cases of shipping damage.

**Exceptions:**
- Damage that occurs after delivery (e.g., customer misuse) is not covered.
- Minor packaging dents that do not affect the product may not qualify for replacement; we will assess each case.

We may reimburse return shipping or send a replacement without requiring return when the damage is obviously due to transit.

## Document 8: Lost Packages

**Short description:**  
What we do when a shipment is lost or missing.

**Policy content:**

If your tracking shows that your package was delivered but you did not receive it, or if tracking has not updated for an extended period, contact us. We will work with the carrier to locate the package or resolve the issue.

**Timeframes:**
- **Domestic:** If there has been no tracking update for **7 business days** after the expected delivery date, we consider the package potentially lost and will open an investigation.
- **International:** Allow **15 business days** after the expected delivery date before we consider a package lost, due to customs and longer transit times.

**Resolution:**
- If the carrier confirms loss or the package is not found, we will offer a **full refund or reshipment** at no extra cost. Reshipment is subject to product availability.
- If the carrier shows "delivered" but you did not receive it, we may require a short investigation (e.g., checking with neighbors, building management). After that, we will typically offer a one-time replacement or refund for first-time occurrences.

**Prevention:** Ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. We are not responsible for packages sent to an incorrect address provided by the customer.

## Document 9: Payment Failures

**Short description:**  
Why a payment might be declined and what to do.

**Policy content:**

If your payment is declined at checkout, the order will not be placed. Common reasons include:

- **Insufficient funds** on the card or account.
- **Card expired or incorrect details** (number, expiry, CVV, or billing address).
- **Bank or card issuer declined** (e.g., fraud prevention, spending limit, or international block).
- **Billing address** does not match the one on file with your bank.

**What to do:**
1. Verify your card number, expiry date, CVV, and billing address.
2. Try another payment method if available.
3. Contact your bank to ensure the card is active and allows online purchases; ask if they blocked the transaction.
4. If the issue persists, contact our customer support. We cannot see the exact decline reason from your bank, but we can suggest alternatives (e.g., different card, PayPal, or splitting payment if we support it).

**Failed payment after order:** If we attempt to charge you for an order (e.g., pre-authorization or subscription) and the payment fails, we will notify you by email. Your order may be put on hold or cancelled if payment is not updated within the timeframe stated in the email.

## Document 10: Warranty

**Short description:**  
Manufacturer and store warranty on products.

**Policy content:**

Most physical products carry a **manufacturer warranty** of 1 year from the date of purchase against defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty details are included in the product packaging or on the product page.

**What is covered:**
- Defects in materials or workmanship under normal use.
- Repair or replacement of the defective product, at the manufacturer’s or our discretion.

**What is not covered:**
- Damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs.
- Normal wear and tear.
- Consumables (e.g., batteries) unless defective.

**How to make a claim:** Contact customer support with your order number and a description of the issue. We may require you to return the item for inspection. If the claim is approved, we will replace the product or refund you according to the warranty terms.

**Extended warranty:** Some items offer optional extended warranty at checkout; terms are provided at the time of purchase.

## Document 11: Promotions and Discounts

How promo codes and discounts work.

Promotions and discount codes can be applied at checkout where indicated. Only one promo code can be used per order unless we explicitly state otherwise (e.g., "stack with free shipping").

## Document 12: Account Issues

**Short description:**  
Password reset, account access, and account changes.

**Policy content:**

You can manage your account from the "Account" section when logged in. For security, we do not disclose account details or make certain changes without verifying your identity.

**Closing an account:** You may request account closure by contacting support. We will process the request and retain only the data we are legally required to keep (e.g., for tax or warranty). Open orders must be completed or cancelled before closure.

## Document 13: Order Modifications

**Short description:**  
Changing address, items, or shipping method after placing an order.

**Policy content:**

Once an order is placed, **changes are only possible if the order is still in Pending status** (not yet shipped). We cannot modify an order after it has been shipped.

**What can be changed (pending orders only):**
- **Shipping address:** Contact us as soon as possible. If the order has not been handed to the carrier, we will update the address. If it has already shipped, we cannot change the destination; you may need to contact the carrier or refuse delivery and place a new order.
- **Shipping method:** We can upgrade or change the shipping method if the order has not yet shipped. You may need to pay the difference for an upgrade.
- **Adding or removing items:** We can add items to the order or remove items if the order has not entered picking/packing. If we remove items, we will refund the difference.

**What cannot be changed:**
- Payment method after the order is placed (you would need to cancel and reorder).
- Modifications after the order has shipped.

Contact customer support with your order number to request changes. We will confirm whether the change is possible.

## Document 21: Opening a support ticket (system)

**Short description:**  
When and how support agents create a row in the **support_tickets** table for the customer.

**Policy content:**

When a customer needs follow-up beyond what you can close in the conversation—ongoing investigations (lost package, damage claim), requests that require another team, or the customer explicitly asks for a ticket—you **open a support ticket** by inserting a record into the **support_tickets** table on their behalf.

**Information to collect from the customer (before creating the ticket):**

- **Customer:** Confirm who you are helping so the ticket is tied to the correct account (resolve **customer_id** from the verified customer; do not guess).
- **Order (when relevant):** If the issue relates to a purchase, obtain the **order number** and resolve it to **order_id**. If the issue is not order-specific (e.g., general account access), **order_id** may be omitted.
- **Subject:** A short, clear **subject** line summarizing the issue (what happened and what they need). This is the main free-text field on the ticket—make it specific enough for the next agent or team to act without re-interviewing the customer.
- **Status:** New tickets must be created with status **Open** unless internal procedures specify otherwise.

**What the system stores:**

- **id** is assigned automatically when the ticket is created.
- **created_at** is set at creation time; **updated_at** may be set when the ticket is first opened or left for later updates when the ticket changes.

**Rules:**

- Only create a ticket after you have the minimum required fields: **customer_id**, **subject**, and **status** (Open). Include **order_id** whenever the problem is tied to a specific order.
- Do not create duplicate tickets for the same open issue in the same conversation; if a ticket already exists for that case, reference it instead.
- Confirm to the customer that a ticket was opened and recap the **subject** (and order, if any) so they know what was recorded.
