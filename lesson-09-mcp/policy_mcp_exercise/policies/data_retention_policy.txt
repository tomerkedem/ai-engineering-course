Data Retention Policy

Company data must be kept only for as long as it is needed for business, legal, or security purposes.

General rules:
- Customer support records are retained for 24 months.
- Security logs are retained for 12 months.
- Temporary files should be deleted when they are no longer needed.
- Employees must not copy company data into personal storage accounts.
- Requests to delete or export data should be sent to the data protection team.
