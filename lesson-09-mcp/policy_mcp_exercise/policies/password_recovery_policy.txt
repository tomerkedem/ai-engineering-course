Password Recovery Policy

Employees who need to recover their password must use the approved password recovery process.

Steps:
- Go to the official password recovery page.
- Verify your identity using the required authentication method.
- Create a new password that meets the company's password requirements.
- Do not reuse an old password.
- If recovery fails, contact the IT help desk.

Employees must never share passwords with another person, including managers or support staff.
