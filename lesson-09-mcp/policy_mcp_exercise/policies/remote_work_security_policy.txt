Remote Work Security Policy

Employees may work remotely only when they follow the company's security requirements.

Required practices:
- Use a company-approved device whenever possible.
- Connect through the approved VPN or secure access solution.
- Do not share your device with other people while working.
- Lock your screen whenever you step away.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi unless you are connected through the approved secure connection.
- Report lost devices, suspicious login attempts, or unusual system behavior immediately.
