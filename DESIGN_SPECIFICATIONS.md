# FIFA World Cup 2026 - Design Specifications

## 🎨 Color Palette

| Name | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
|------|----------|-----|-------|
| **Deep Navy** | `#0C1E3C` | RGB(12, 30, 60) | Primary background - professional base |
| **Bright Gold** | `#FFD700` | RGB(255, 215, 0) | Titles, accents - premium feel |

## 📐 Layout Specifications

### Slide Dimensions
- **Format:** 16:9 Widescreen
- **Width:** 10 inches
- **Height:** 5.625 inches
- **Margins:** 0.5" minimum on all sides

### Typography

#### Headers
- **Font:** Arial Black
- **Sizes:** 
  - Main titles: 44-54pt
  - Section headers: 24-36pt
  - Card titles: 20-24pt
- **Weight:** Bold
- **Color:** Gold (#FFD700) or White (#FFFFFF) depending on background

#### Body Text
- **Font:** Arial
- **Sizes:**
  - Body text: 14-16pt
  - Captions: 12pt
  - Small text: 10pt
- **Color:** Dark Gray (#1F2937) or White (#FFFFFF)
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned for paragraphs, centered for headers

#### Callouts & Statistics
- **Font:** Arial Black
- **Number size:** 44-54pt (bold)
- **Label size:** 12-14pt
- **Color:** White text on colored backgrounds

---

## 🎯 Design Elements

### Accent Bars
- **Width:** 0.08" - 0.15" (decorative lines)
- **Colors:** Rotate between Orange, Gold, Red, Navy
- **Placement:** 
  - Top/bottom of slides
  - Left side of titles
  - Between sections
- **Purpose:** Visual separation and emphasis

### Stat Cards
- **Dimensions:** 4.25" × 1" (for 2x2 grid layout)
- **Background:** Solid color (Orange, Red, Navy, or Light Blue)
- **Border:** 2pt colored border (Orange, Gold, Red)
- **Text alignment:** Center, middle-aligned
- **Padding:** 0.2" internal margin

### Feature Cards
- **Dimensions:** 4.25" × 1.6"
- **Background:** Light Sky Blue (#E0F2FE)
- **Border:** 2pt Orange (#FF6B35)
- **Corner radius:** Sharp (rectangular)
- **Title:** 14pt bold, Dark Blue
- **Description:** 12pt regular, Dark Gray
- **Padding:** 0.2" internal margin

### Content Boxes (Host Nations)
- **Dimensions:** 2.8" × 4"
- **Header section:** 0.7" height with colored background
- **Body section:** White background with bullet points
- **Colors:** 
  - USA: Vibrant Red
  - Mexico: Electric Orange
  - Canada: Deep Navy

## 🎭 Visual Hierarchy

### Primary (Highest Emphasis)
- Main titles in Gold, 44-54pt, bold
- Large statistics (44-54pt numbers)
- Colored stat/feature cards with high contrast

### Secondary (Medium Emphasis)
- Section headers (24-36pt)
- Colored accent bars and borders
- Supporting statistics

### Tertiary (Lower Emphasis)
- Body text (14-16pt)
- Bullet points
- Captions and small text

---

## 🖼️ Visual Motifs

### Repeated Elements
1. **Accent Bars** - Orange, Gold, Red, Navy bars appear on every slide
2. **Color-Coded Sections** - Consistent use of colors for different topics
3. **Stat Callouts** - Large numbers with supporting text
4. **Structural Lines** - Horizontal and vertical lines for organization

### Design Consistency
- ✅ Header bars consistent across slides
- ✅ Margin spacing standardized (0.5")
- ✅ Font sizes follow hierarchy
- ✅ Color palette restricted to 7 core colors
- ✅ No jarring visual changes between slides

## 🎨 Color Psychology in Sports Broadcasting

- **Navy (#0C1E3C):** Trust, professionalism, stability (primary background)
- **Gold (#FFD700):** Excellence, prestige, champions (titles & accents)
- **Orange (#FF6B35):** Energy, excitement, enthusiasm (highlights)
- **Red (#EF4444):** Passion, action, sports intensity (callouts)
- **White (#FFFFFF):** Clarity, cleanliness, openness (contrast)
- **Light Blue (#E0F2FE):** Calm, approachability (content areas)

This palette reflects the excitement of world-class sports while maintaining professional credibility.
